About me

I am a third year PhD student at Crest, ENSAE, Institut Polytechnique de Paris and Istituto Italiano di Tecnologia (IIT), Genova, where I am fortunate to be advised by Alexandre B. Tsybakov and Massimiliano Pontil. I am interested in optimization in all aspects and mainly its applications in statistical inference.

In 2018, I obtained my master's degree in Mathematics, Machine Learning, Sciences, and Humanities (MASH) from Paris Dauphine University and École Normale Supérieure Paris-Saclay with highest honors, mention très bien.
